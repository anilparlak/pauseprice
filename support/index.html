Frequently Asked Questions

How does PausePrice calculate my hourly value?

Your hourly value = (Monthly income - Monthly expenses) / Monthly work hours

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

How do I change my currency?

Go to Profile → Currency → Select your preferred currency (TRY, USD, EUR, GBP).

How do I cancel my subscription?
  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → PausePrice → Cancel
  • Android: Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → PausePrice → Cancel
How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile → Reset All Data. This permanently deletes all your decisions and settings.

The camera can't read my price tag. What should I do?

Hold the tag flat, make sure there's enough light, and keep the price numbers clearly visible within the scan frame.
