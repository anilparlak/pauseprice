Privacy Policy for PausePrice

Last updated: March 31, 2026

PausePrice ("we", "our", or "the app") is a purchase reflection tool that converts prices into hours of your life.

Data We Collect

PausePrice stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Monthly income, expenses, and working hours (for hourly rate calculation)
  • Purchase decisions and their status
  • App preferences (language, currency)

Camera Usage

PausePrice uses your device camera solely to scan price tags using on-device OCR (optical character recognition). Camera images are processed locally and are never uploaded or stored.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data as described in their privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs

Data Deletion

All app data can be deleted using the "Reset Data" option in the Profile screen. Uninstalling the app also removes all data.

Children's Privacy

PausePrice is not directed at children under 13.
